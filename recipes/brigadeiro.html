Brigadeiro

Description

A delicious Brazilian dessert that is easy to prepare and makes for a great snack for parties

Ingredients

  • 1 box of condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon of saltless margarine
  • 7 tablespoons of chocolate powder
  • chocolate sprinkles

Cooking process

  1. Put the condensed milk, chocolate powder and margarine inside a deep pan
  2. Cook on a median flame and stir until it's no longer sticking to the pan
  3. Let it cool down then roll into small balls and cover with sprinkles
