Lasagna

Description

One of the most well-known and beloved dishes from Italy. A time consuming but delicious recipe that will make any dinner fantastic.

Ingredients

  • sausage
  • ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • lasagna noodles
  • cheese (Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta)
  • 1 egg
  • parsley
  • dried basil leaves
  • salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • fennel seeds
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. make the meat sauce
  2. cook the noodles
  3. make the ricotta mixture
  4. layer the lasagna in the order(meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella slices, meat sauce, Parmesan cheese)
  5. cover with foil and bake
  6. let the lasagna rest before serving
